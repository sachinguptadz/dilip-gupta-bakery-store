Meet the Owner
"Hamari bakery mein jo cheez banati hai woh machine nahi, haath hain — aur unme 25 saal ka pyaar hai."

FAQ

Aapke Sawaal,
Hamare Jawaab

Custom cakes aur orders ke baare mein common questions ke jawaab yahan hain.

Custom cake ka order kaise karein?+
WhatsApp par +91 7071878792 pe message karein — cake ka flavour (chocolate, vanilla, butterscotch, red velvet, black forest), size (500g se 2kg), aur koi special design ya message batayein. Hum price aur ready time confirm karenge.
Same-day cake kab tak ready ho jaata hai?+
Agar order subah 10 baje tak aa jaaye toh cake sham 5-6 baje tak ready ho jaata hai. Rush orders ke liye phone par pehle confirm karein. Shop se pickup karna hoga.
Kya aap home delivery karte hain?+
Abhi hum home delivery nahi karte — sirf shop se pickup available hai. Hamare WhatsApp par order karke apna pickup time confirm karein.
Bakery cookies aur brand biscuits mein kya farkh hai?+
Hamare bakery cookies roz fresh bake hote hain — no preservatives, no packaging. Butter cookies, atta cookies, coconut cookies sab fresh mein milte hain. Taste mein brand biscuits se kahi zyada better hain.
Bulk ya event orders accept karte hain?+
Haan! School canteen, office events, birthday parties ke liye bulk orders accepted hain. ₹500 se upar ke orders par special rates milenge. Pehle se 1 din advance notice dein.
Payment ke kya options hain?+
Cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) sab accept karte hain. Custom cake orders mein advance payment required hoti hai.
